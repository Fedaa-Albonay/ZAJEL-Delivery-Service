Frequently Asked Questions

For Abu Dhabi, we offer same-day delivery. Other emirates usually take 24 to 48 hours.

Delivery times depend on the destination and service type. We strive to meet your expectations with every shipment.

How can I track my shipment?

You can use the tracking section on our website by entering your ZAJ-ID number.

Do you provide delivery insurance?

Yes, all parcels handled by Zajel are insured against loss or damage during transit.

What items are prohibited from shipping?

We do not ship hazardous materials, chemicals, or illegal substances according to UAE law.

What are your working hours?

Our delivery team works 24/7, and our customer support is available from 8 AM to 10 PM.
